Bootstrap 5 Tooltip and Popover Examples

Example 1: Tooltip
<button type="button" class="btn btn-secondary" data-bs-toggle="tooltip" data-bs-placement="top" title="Tooltip on top">
  Hover over me
</button>
Example 2: Popover
<button type="button" class="btn btn-lg btn-danger" data-bs-toggle="popover" title="Popover title" data-bs-content="And here's some amazing content. It's very engaging. Right?">
  Click to toggle popover
</button>
Example 3: Popover with Placement
<button type="button" class="btn btn-secondary" data-bs-toggle="popover" data-bs-placement="right" title="Popover title" data-bs-content="And here's some amazing content. It's very engaging. Right?">
  Popover on right
</button>
